Production Brain

The central intelligence of your workspace, the Production Brain (Workspace Memory) passively compiles your organization's history. It learns from your past budgets, freelancer rates, task durations, and resource bookings to build a secure, private knowledge base. When you use our scoping or matchmaking tools, the AI queries this brain to deliver smart, customized recommendations tailored precisely to how your team works.

Advanced Film Production Tools

ABRAM replaces scattered spreadsheets with unified, interactive tools for physical and creative production:

  • AI Script Breakdown: Upload script PDFs to instantly tag characters, props, and scene elements, mapping location requirements automatically.
  • Stripboards & Scheduling: Easily drag-and-drop scene strips, track calendar timelines, and match shooting dates to actor availability.
  • Interactive Call Sheets: Generate and distribute official production call sheets with locations, weather, and schedules, integrated with Slack check-ins so crew can mark themselves on-site.
